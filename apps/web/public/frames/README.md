# Preset frames (worn around the avatar — use transparent PNGs)

Drop shop images here (png/jpg/jpeg/webp), any names — listed alphabetically.
Bought with coins in the shop. Restart or refresh the shop to pick up new files.

## Art spec (so the pfp fits the inner circle perfectly)

- Canvas: **square 1:1**, 1024×1024 recommended, transparent background.
- The app renders the frame at exactly the avatar's box and scales the pfp to
  **70%** inside it. So design with a **centered transparent circle of
  diameter ≈ 70% of the canvas** (≈ 716 px on 1024) — the pfp shows through it
  edge-to-edge.
- Ring artwork lives between 70% and ~98% of the canvas; keep a small 1–2%
  outer margin so nothing touches the edge.
- Decorations may overlap slightly *into* the circle (they'll sit on top of
  the pfp) — that's fine and looks intentional; just keep the face area clear.
